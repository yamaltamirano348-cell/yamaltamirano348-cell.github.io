Brand Concept & Mission

Paws & Polish Grooming is a pet grooming service focused on gentle, professional care for dogs and cats. Our mission is to provide a calm, stress-free grooming experience while ensuring pets look and feel their best.

Color Scheme

  • Soft Mint (#A8E6CF) — accents and highlights
  • Warm Taupe (#6B5B4D) — body text
  • Cream White (#FAFAF7) — backgrounds
  • Charcoal (#2F2F2F) — headings

Font Choices

Headings: Trebuchet MS — used for titles and section headers

Body Text: Arial / Helvetica — used for paragraphs and lists

Tagline

Because every pet deserves to feel pampered.
